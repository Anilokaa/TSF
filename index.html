Who are we?

Loka's Foundation is committed to help the most vulnerable in these unprecedented times.

In the wake of the coronavirus pandemic, Loka's Foundation is seeing micro businesses collapse and families lose their only source of income. Join us as we send lifesaving help to our entrepreneurs so that they can get back on their feet.
